SRI ESHWAR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

About Sri eshwar college

Remarks :
The most important is the reputed college around Coimbatore. The surrounding is neat and tidy and also the hostel is really good as I expected. The distance also is nearby but still I'm in boarding. The college has got the experienced teaching staffs and also most important is the college has gone tie up with the most of the companies in and around Coimbatore.
Course Curriculum Overview :
  The institute is affiliated to Anna University and the course offered are up to date. There are lots of machine learning activities and also lots of competitions are offered in and out of the College. The College has got the latest innovations and technologies done by the students by themselves.
Placement Experience :
  Our college got got direct placements in top companies like Bosch Ltd, Virtusa,CSS CORP, HP global,Marico Industries Ltd and more. The company provides a package of 5l. 3 students from 3rd year have been placed through their Hackathon Codelite with a package of 6l /annum. One student of 2021 batch is placed in ACIES salary of 3.75 L PA. 19 students got placed in Capgemini salary of 3.80 L PA and 16 students placed in unschool salary 5L PA. 3 students placed in Beez innovation with a salary of 3L PA.
College Events :
  The college has done with lots of industry connect programs recently through online and it is done for more than 5 years where each and every college around Coimbatore can participate and execute their projects. The college has opened media max a massive club for the talents for singing,dancing, photography and more. The opening of the media max was done by a celebrity and lots of programs have been conducted on behalf of the media max team to enhance more talents. The functions are conducted in the auditorium with a very good progress and all the events have been a great success because of the students cooperation.
Loan/ Scholarship Provisions :
  I'm unaware about it. But the college affords scholarship for single girl child. The college also provides scholarship for the students who has scored 85% above in their higher schools.
Campus Life :
   The campus is located in a tranquil and conducive ambience creating an ideal learning environment to stimulate intellectual and personal growth. It has got a medium sized library with the 10000 sqFt IT CENTRE. The college gives equal importance for both sports and learning. The college laboratory has the latest innovations, many club activities are also conducted by the college for the betterment of the students.
Facilities :
   The hostel is such a neat and tidy place, with a peaceful environment. The college has got separate hostel facilities for both boys and girls. The college provides gym for both boys and girls separately. The hostel food is good and the room is so spacious and also you can ask for separate rooms if your not comfortable with 4 people. The hostel rooms can have six members a larger one and also three people and also with one in a room. In case if you are not comfortable with your room mates you can ask the warden for further process. The hostel provides 24/7 hot water and there is no problem with the hygienic issues. The girls can feel as home in this hostel and peacefully you can learn.
